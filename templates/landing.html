How it works

Four steps to your
dream team

From idea to collaboration in minutes. No gatekeeping, no fees, no drama.

01 / 04
✍️
Post your project
Describe what you're building, set the duration, choose categories, and list the roles you need — as many as you want.
02 / 04
🔍
Browse and discover
Search and filter projects by type, pay, time commitment, and more. Find what genuinely excites you.
03 / 04
📩
Apply to a role
Write a cover letter, upload your resume, and apply to the specific role that fits your skills perfectly.
04 / 04
🤝
Get accepted
Go through shortlisting and an interview. Once accepted, both sides get each other's contact details.
STAGE 01

STAGE 04

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for students, developers, and creators who want to collaborate without the noise.

⚡
One account, two roles
You're not just a creator or just an applicant — you're both. Post your own project and apply to others at the same time, all from a single unified dashboard. Your posted projects and your applications live side by side, always in one place.
Unified dashboard Post and apply No switching accounts
🎯
Role-specific applications
You don't apply to a whole project — you apply to a specific role. Frontend Dev, UI/UX Designer, Data Analyst. Your application goes exactly where it belongs.
Targeted Precise
📅
Built-in interview pipeline
Shortlist candidates, schedule interviews with date, time, and a Google Meet link — all from within the platform. Applicants get notified by email at every single stage.
Email alerts Interview scheduling
🔒
Privacy by design
Contact details — email and phone — are hidden until a creator accepts an applicant. No unsolicited contact. No spam. Just meaningful connections at the right moment.
Secure Anti-spam
🔍
Smart search and filters
Search by keyword and filter by Tech / Non-tech, Paid / Unpaid, Part-time / Full-time. Find exactly the kind of project you're looking for in seconds.
6 filter types Keyword search
📄
Resume per application
Upload a fresh, tailored resume for each application — different from your profile resume. Because every role deserves your best, most relevant version.
PDF upload Per-role resumes
